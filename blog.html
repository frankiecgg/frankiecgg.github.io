JavaScript Library Created by My Own

Posted on 10 March 2021  by Siyuan Chen

It is the a Javascript Library that allows user to interact the website with mouse gesture.

This library provides webdevelopers access to allow users to do some interactions with the website by using mouse gesture.

Users can easily hold left button to draw a line right or left in a specified area to activate the corresponding actions.

Meanwhile, developers can also define their own responses to a mouse gesture by their will.

At this moment, the supported mouse gestures are "to right", "to left", "up", and "down".

CryptoGoGo: A Virtual Currency Trading Simulation Platform

Posted on 17 March 2021  by Siyuan Chen

This is the project that made by my team in CSC309, we design and produced this web app to help investors to gain experiance in crypto cur­ rency trading.

This application will be targeted to new investors who is trying to build confidence in trading crypto currencies.

We want to help educate new investors on the psycologies on trading with practical ex­ periance as well as technical knowledge, before they step into the shark tank with well experianced traders.

The users will be able to test out strategies and theories on our app with a trading simulation platform.

We will be providing each users with data, accounts, and starting currencies to pratice trading.

The users will also be albe to do analysis and comparsions.

We will provide data and charting tools for the users to use to track their holdings.

Lastly, the user will be able to share their theories and analysis to get comments and feedbacks from other traders.

This will be compelted with a implementation of a discussion board to post analyzed charts.

On the high level, we seek to implement ideas and philosophies to even the playing ground for new self directed investors and experianced professionals in crypto currency trading.
